About Spectrum

Spectrum, the premier professional skill development club at KUET, serves as a vital bridge between academic theory and industry-ready competence. As the dedicated club partner and host of the Hult Prize, Spectrum cultivates a culture of social entrepreneurship on campus.

Flagship Events

  • JobSpecs 2025 – KUET’s first national job fair
  • Hult Prize Campus Round – Grooming sessions, mentorship workshops, Grand Finale

Impact

Spectrum has supported hundreds of startups and helped KUET teams secure top positions in international regional finals.
